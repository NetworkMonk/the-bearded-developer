# Content Strategy — March 2026
**Period:** 3 March – 2 April 2026
**Primary SEO targets:** Celigo · BigCommerce · Digital Support Retainers

---

## Strategy Overview

The content foundation is now strong. The focus for the next 30 days is:

1. **Fill the highest-value keyword gaps** — particularly BigCommerce cost/pricing and Celigo pricing (both high commercial intent, both missing)
2. **Deepen the Celigo and BigCommerce clusters** — Google rewards topic authority; more interlinked content on the same subject increases rankings across all articles in that cluster
3. **Convert readers into leads** — articles should always connect to a service page or contact CTA
4. **Distribute what already exists** — LinkedIn and community posts cost no extra effort and compound article value

Target cadence: **2 articles per week** (achievable with Claude's help). Non-article tasks sit alongside, not instead of, article work.
